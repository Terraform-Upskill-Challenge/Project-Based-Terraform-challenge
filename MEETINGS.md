# Biweekly Meeting Structure

## Meeting Overview

**Frequency**: Every 2 weeks  
**Duration**: 60-90 minutes  
**Format**: Live coding, Q&A, peer review

---

## Meeting Agenda Template

### 1. Welcome & Check-in (10 minutes)
- Quick round of introductions
- What did you accomplish in the last 2 weeks?
- What challenges did you face?
- One thing you're proud of

### 2. Topic Deep Dive (30-40 minutes)
- Live coding walkthrough
- Best practices discussion
- Common pitfalls and how to avoid them
- Q&A on the topic

### 3. Code Review Session (20 minutes)
- Review participant code (volunteer-based)
- Discuss different approaches
- Learn from each other's solutions
- Identify improvements

### 4. Q&A & Troubleshooting (15-20 minutes)
- Open floor for questions
- Troubleshoot specific issues
- Share resources and tips
- Plan for next 2 weeks

### 5. Wrap-up (5 minutes)
- Assignments for next meeting
- Resources to review
- Next meeting date/time

## Meeting Best Practices

### For Participants:
1. **Come Prepared**: Complete assigned tasks before meeting
2. **Ask Questions**: No question is too basic
3. **Share Code**: Be ready to share your solutions
4. **Take Notes**: Document key learnings
5. **Engage**: Help others when you can

### For Facilitator:
1. **Start on Time**: Respect everyone's schedule
2. **Be Patient**: Everyone learns at different paces
3. **Encourage Participation**: Create safe space for questions
4. **Provide Resources**: Share links and documentation
5. **Follow Up**: Send meeting notes and resources
